Privacy Policy

MusicMakr — Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

MusicMakr is a music and video creation tool. This privacy policy explains what data MusicMakr collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Generated Content

Content created through MusicMakr (lyrics, audio files, video files) is stored locally on your device. MusicMakr does not collect, store, or transmit your generated content to any server controlled by us.

Social Media Account Access

When you connect MusicMakr to social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok), MusicMakr uses OAuth 2.0 to obtain access tokens scoped to content uploading. MusicMakr:

  • Only requests permissions necessary for uploading content
  • Stores OAuth tokens locally on your device
  • Does not access your social media data beyond what is required for posting
  • Does not read your followers, messages, or engagement data

Usage Data

MusicMakr does not collect analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or any usage data.

Third-Party Services

MusicMakr integrates with third-party services for content creation and distribution. When you use these services through MusicMakr, your data is sent directly to the respective provider. Each service is governed by its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to any MusicMakr-operated server
  • You are responsible for securing access to your device

Children's Privacy

MusicMakr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since MusicMakr does not collect or store your data on any remote server, there is no personal data for us to delete, export, or modify. All data remains under your control on your local device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the MusicMakr GitHub repository.
